// LATEST MILESTONE
Storm Role — First Playable
Electric arc abilities now in prototype. Feels dangerous. Needs tuning.
Week 47 · Nov 2025
Storm Role — First Playable Prototype
Got Storm's core ability loop into the build. The electromagnetic pulse works — disables nearby enemy abilities for 2.5s. Feels powerful but needs range tuning. Electric arc visuals are chef's kiss though.
Build
Week 45 · Nov 2025
Stealth Rework — Cloak Mechanic v2
Old stealth was too binary. New system: partial transparency with a shimmer effect visible at close range. Rewards good positioning over just pressing a button. Way more fun to play against.
Design
Week 43 · Oct 2025
Dung Roll Physics — Major Overhaul
The old roll felt like pushing a block. New system uses actual momentum accumulation — speed builds up on downhill terrain. Completely changes escort meta. Love it.
Build
Week 41 · Oct 2025
Role Art Pass — All 6 Concept Sheets Done
Finished the full concept art pass for all six roles. Defender got a shield-arm redesign. Storm got bioluminescent accents on the carapace. Stealth shell is now oil-slick iridescent.
Art
Week 38 · Sep 2025
5v5 Playtest #3 — The Fun Breakthrough
A Trapper laid a maze of snares around the central pile, Scout called the flanks, two Attackers dove in and stole the roll mid-escort. Absolute chaos. This is the game.
