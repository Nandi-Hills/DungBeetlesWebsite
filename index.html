// The Worldview

THE MICRO
BATTLEFIELD

Welcome to the chaotic, vibrant, nature-inspired world of Dung Beetles. You don't control supersoldiers or conquerors — you control determined, resilient scavengers surviving in an oversized world.

Two factions clash across a nature-scale arena. Every blade of grass is a wall. Every pebble is a fortress. Every pile of dung is victory itself.

The core fantasy lives in agility, instinct, and teamwork — and the chaos of competing for your most precious resource.

// Core Loop

HOW IT'S PLAYED

01

Harvest

Venture across the battlefield to collect small dung pickups or fight for the highly contested central dung pile. Every gram matters.

02

Roll

Seize the central source and physically build massive dung rolls. Enter the universal Carrier State — trade your abilities for raw Momentum.

03

Escort

Your teammates become your shield. Coordinate escort formations, body-block enemy interceptors, and punch through their defensive lines.

04

Bank

Store resources at your home base to climb the score. Invest in turrets, walls, traps, or observation beetles. Defend what you've earned.

// Roster

FOUR ROLES,
INFINITE PLAY

Role 01

Defender

Masters of territory. Defenders anchor your base perimeter, manage turret deployment, and intercept enemy carriers mid-roll with precision knockback.

Role 02

Attacker

Born to disrupt. Attackers slash into enemy formations, strip carried dung from opponents, and create chaos that turns the tide of any escort.

Role 03

Scout

Information is the ultimate weapon. Scouts reveal threats before they manifest, track enemy positioning, and open flanking lanes through dark terrain.

Role 04

Trapper

Patience is a weapon. Trappers lay hidden snares along choke points, redirect enemy traffic, and create devastating kill zones around key objectives.

// Resource Economy

ONE RESOURCE.
THREE PURPOSES.

Dung is not just what you're fighting for — it's everything. It's your score, your ammunition, and your wallet. Every gram you collect forces a strategic decision.

  • Victory Points — Bank at your home base to climb toward the target score. Matches end when time expires or a team hits the limit.
  • Ability Fuel — Spend stored dung to power ranged attacks and special abilities. High action drains your reserves.
  • Base Currency — Invest in turrets, perimeter walls, observation beetles, and trap networks. Every credit spent is dung not banked.
// Design Philosophy OBJECTIVE
OVER KILL

Combat exists purely to interrupt enemy carriers, protect allies, reveal threats, and punish overextension. Knocking out an opponent simply forces them to drop their dung and respawn — creating tactical openings, not kill streaks.
